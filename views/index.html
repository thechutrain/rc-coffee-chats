A Coffee Chats bot for Zulip

Oh hi,

This is the backend for a bot allowing current participants of the Recurse Center to get matched for coffee with each other.

To get matched, subscribe yourself to the Coffee Chats Zulip channel before 10am on any given day. We'll find you someone to meet up with!

Coffee isn't required - it could be tea, or snacks, or just a walk - and you don't even have to leave the building if you just want to catch up in the kitchen or somewhere else on the 4th floor.

If you're sick that day and forget to remove yourself, don't worry! You can just meet with the person you paired with once you're back.

Feel free to add or remove yourself at any point, (just try to do so before 9am if you don't want to have coffee that day). Thanks and happy chatting!
